About the Campaign

The #AerieREAL campaign was launched by Aerie (American Eagle’s lingerie and lifestyle brand) in 2014 with the mission to redefine beauty standards in the fashion and intimates industry. The "Real Me" line, an extension of this campaign, focuses on comfort, confidence, and authenticity.

  • No Retouching: Aerie made a bold promise to stop retouching models in their ads. They proudly show stretch marks, scars, cellulite, and natural features.
  • Diverse Representation: The campaign highlights models of all body types, abilities, ethnicities, and gender identities — including people with chronic illnesses, disabilities, and varying skin tones.
  • The “Real Me” Collection: A soft, lightweight fabric line designed to feel like a second skin — it’s about both physical comfort and emotional confidence.
  • REAL Voices & Aerie Changemakers: Aerie uplifts community leaders and everyday people with inspiring stories, giving them a platform to promote self-love, mental health, and body confidence.
  • Philanthropy: Through the Aerie Real Foundation, the brand supports nonprofits focused on youth empowerment, education, and wellness.
